Privacy Policy

Extension: Microsoft to Pedosoft Replacer

Effective date: 2026-02-05

This policy explains what information the extension can access and how it is handled.

What the extension does

The extension is a satirical text replacer. It runs as a Chrome content script and replaces certain predefined words/phrases on webpages you visit by scanning text nodes in the page and updating the displayed text. It skips scripts, styles, and typical user input elements (such as input, textarea, and editable content).

Where it runs

The extension is configured to run on pages that match <all_urls>. This means it may operate on any webpage you open in your browser in order to perform the text replacements.

Information the extension may access

To replace text, the extension may read and modify the text content displayed on the webpage (the page’s DOM text nodes). This processing occurs locally on your device inside your browser.

Data collection

The extension does not collect personal information.

Data transmission

The extension does not transmit data to the developer or to third parties. It does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, telemetry, or remote logging, and it does not make network requests for data collection.

Data storage

The extension does not store browsing history or webpage content. It also does not store user settings in its current version. If a future version adds optional settings storage (for example via chrome.storage), it will store only user preferences needed for functionality.

Third-party services

The extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children’s privacy

The extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date.
